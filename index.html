Hello!

I am a 33-year old postdoc in Gold Lab at the University of Pennsylvania. Prior to this, I did a Ph.D. at the University of Houston in Mathematics applied to Bayes-optimal decision making involving temporal integration of evidence. My advisors were Krešimir Josić and Zachary P. Kilpatrick.

My current research aims at running psychophysics tasks to investigate the way in which humans accumulate evidence in changing environments.
